# Data Sharing Statement

The analysis code, processed outputs and publication assets for this project are publicly available at:

https://github.com/hssling/injury-india-public-health

The repository includes:

- analysis scripts for ingestion, cleaning, analysis, quality checks and figure generation
- processed summary outputs used in the manuscript
- submission-ready publication assets under `outputs/publication/`

Primary source data remain subject to their original providers:

- GBD results must be obtained from the IHME GBD Results Tool
- MoRTH road safety reports must be obtained from the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways
- NCRB reports must be obtained from the National Crime Records Bureau

No individual-level participant data are contained in this repository.
