# Declarations

## Ethics Approval and Consent to Participate
This study used exclusively publicly available, de-identified, aggregated secondary data obtained from: (1) the IHME Global Health Data Exchange (GBD 2021); (2) the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, Government of India (Road Accidents in India 2023); and (3) the National Crime Records Bureau, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India (Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India 2023). No individual-level data were collected, accessed, or analysed. Institutional ethics approval was therefore not required.

## Consent for Publication
Not applicable.

## Availability of Data and Materials
The primary datasets used in this analysis are freely and publicly available:
- GBD 2021 estimates: https://ghdx.healthdata.org/gbd-results (free registration required)
- MoRTH Road Accidents in India 2023: https://road.transport.gov.in
- NCRB Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India 2023: https://ncrb.gov.in

All analysis code is available from the corresponding author on reasonable request. The project repository includes scripts for data parsing, harmonization, analysis, and figure generation.
